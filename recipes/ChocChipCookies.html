The Best Choc Chip Cookies Recipe

Description

If you are wanting to find the best Choc Chip Cookie Recipe there is, look no further. I am about to show you the last recipe you will ever need that will always be a show stopper.

Ingedients

  • 500g unsalted butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 cups plain flour
  • 200 grams dark chocolate (chooped)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees celsius.
  2. Beat butter and sugars until fluffy.
  3. Add vanilla and then beat in eggs one at a time.
  4. Sift flour and baking powder into wet mixture.
  5. Mix until just combined to form dough, add chooped chocolate.
  6. Bake cookies for 12 mintues or until just golden.
  7. Serve warm for best results.
